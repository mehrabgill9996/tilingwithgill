Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Tiles With Gill ("we," "us," or "our") is a small tiling business based in Brampton, Ontario. This page explains, in plain language, how we handle the personal information you share with us through our website's contact and quote request forms.

What We Collect

When you fill out our contact form, quote request form, or feedback form, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and any details you share about your tiling project or experience with us.

How We Use Your Information

We only use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry, prepare a quote, schedule work, or follow up about a project. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Protect Your Information

Form submissions are transmitted securely and are only accessed by Mehrab Singh Gill in order to respond to your request. We take reasonable steps to keep your information safe, in line with the general principles of Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

Your Rights

You can request a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it at any time. To make a request, simply contact us directly using the details below and we'll take care of it promptly.

Cookies & Analytics

This website does not currently use tracking cookies or third-party analytics beyond what may be required for basic site functionality (such as embedded Google Maps).
