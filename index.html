About Us

Meet Mehrab Singh Gill

Tiles with Gill is owned and operated by Mehrab Singh Gill, a dedicated tiling specialist serving Brampton and the Greater Toronto Area. With years of hands-on experience in residential tiling, Mehrab has built a reputation for clean lines, precise cuts, and finishes that last.

Specializing in washroom and bathroom tiling, every project is treated with the same attention to detail — whether it's a full bathroom renovation, a new kitchen backsplash, or waterproofing a wet area. Quality, reliability, and honest communication are at the core of every job.
